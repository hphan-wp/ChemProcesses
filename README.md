# ChemProcesses
A growing library of chemical process models, plant recreations, and engineering studies built for learning and experimentation.

## Overview
**ChemProcesses** is a collection of educational chemical process models and plant recreations developed to explore process engienering, simulation, and design. The models are based on publicly available information, engineering assumptions, and literature where applicable. **This repository serves as a personal learning porfolio documenting my chemical engineering and process modeling.**

## Disclaimer
These models are intended for **educational puposes only** and do not represent validated industrial designs. Simplications and assumptions have been made through each project, and model fidelity will vary depending on the concepts being studied and my level of understanding at the time of development.
